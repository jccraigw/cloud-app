About

Welcome, I'm Julian Craigwell-Graham and I enjoy creating and building tools that help or entertain others. I completed my undergrad at North Carolina State University studying textile engineering but due to the direction of the industry, I decided to pursue a career in computer programming. This lead me back to North Carolina State to complete their computer programming certification program. Since then I've been an application developer at SAS Institute for almost a decade working with various financial system tools. However, during this time I was given the opportunity to build a few web applications and immediately knew where I wanted my career to go next. The web development space gave me the opportunity to build helpful, fun, or entertaining applications that I could share with others. With my new-found passion, I next decided to attend General Assembly where I would learn full stack web development. The projects you see below demonstrate my ability to learn how to learn quickly and my love for coding.
