About

Welcome! I am Julian Craigwell-Graham and I enjoy creating and building applications that help to support and entertain others. I completed my undergraduate degree in Textile Engineering at North Carolina State University. Due to the changes in the textile industry, which no longer met my needs, I decided to change my career and pursue a career in computer programming. North Carolina State University had a dynamic program that gave me a strong academic foundation to pursue my interest in computer programming. I earned a graduate certificate in computer programming while working full time at SAS Institute where I had been employed for nearly a decade. At SAS Institute, I served as an application developer, working with various financial system tools such as Oracle and Dynamics.

Fortunately, while at SAS Institute, I decided to challenge myself by building a few web applications and immediately knew where I wanted my career to go next. I began to research this field and learned that as a web developer, I could build essential, engaging, and entertaining applications that I could market and share with others. I became completely committed to pursuing my desire to become a web developer. I left my job at the SAS Institute and relocated to attend General Assembly where I was sure that I would gain the skills needed to pursue full stack web development. The projects below demonstrate my ability, the skills I gained, and my passion for web development.
